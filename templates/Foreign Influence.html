Russian Influence



The major concern with political ads running on Facebook has been its susceptibility to foreign influence.According to Facebook’s policy, foreign entities outside of the US can run targeted ads in the US.


The house intelligence committee, as part of the investigation into Russian interference in 2016 released ads that were identified to have been run by Internet Research Agency – a Russian company engaged in online influence operations on behalf of Russian business and political interests. 3,519 ads were purchased by the company and were promoted on 470 Facebook pages. These ads were seen by 11.4 million Americans.


Our team analyzed this data in hopes of finding trends. First, we wanted to know where the ads were targeted and compared it to the battleground states map for the 2016 election cycle.




Source: Ballotpedia

Our team also wanted to see what words were used in this campaign to see if there was a trend.Based on the word cloud, most of the ads covered divisive issues such as the black lives matter movement.
